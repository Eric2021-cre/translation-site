📂 Portfolio & Translation Samples

Here are some real examples showing the quality difference between raw AI output and human-reviewed translation.

Before / After: AI Translation → Human Review

Sample 1: Business Email (Chinese → Japanese)

Source (Simplified Chinese):

您好！感谢您在百忙之中抽出时间来参加我们的会议。关于您提出的方案，我们已经进行了详细的讨论，总体来说方向是可以接受的，但有几个细节需要进一步确认。

AI Translation (Before):

Hello! Thank you for taking time out of your busy schedule to attend our meeting. Regarding the proposal you submitted, we have had detailed discussions. The overall direction is acceptable, but some details need further confirmation.

After Human Review:

Thank you for taking time from your busy schedule to attend our meeting. We have carefully reviewed the proposal you submitted and find the overall direction acceptable. However, there are a few points we would like to confirm before proceeding.

What was improved:

  • Removed casual "Hello!" — inappropriate for business correspondence
  • "some details need further confirmation" → more natural, professional phrasing
  • Tone aligned with formal business communication norms

Sample 2: Literary Prose (Traditional Chinese → Japanese)

Source (Traditional Chinese):

秋風吹過，樹葉開始變黃，我忽然想起了故鄉的那條老街，街邊的桂花香味，還有媽媽做的飯菜。

AI Translation (Before):

The autumn wind blew, the leaves began to turn yellow, and I suddenly remembered the old street in my hometown, the osmanthus scent along the street, and my mother's cooking.

After Human Review:

As the autumn wind passed through, leaves began to color. I found myself thinking of that old lane back home — the sweet scent of osmanthus drifting along the roadside, and the warmth of my mother's cooking.
